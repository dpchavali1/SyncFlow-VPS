SyncFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

SyncFlow collects the following data to provide cross-device SMS synchronization:

  • SMS and MMS messages (content, sender, recipient, timestamps, attachments)
  • Contacts (names, phone numbers) for display in the messaging interface
  • Call history (phone numbers, call type, duration, timestamps)
  • Phone number associated with your device SIM card
  • Device information (device model, OS version, unique device identifier for pairing)
  • Clipboard content when clipboard sync is explicitly enabled by the user
  • Notification content (app name, title, text) when notification mirroring is explicitly enabled by the user
  • Files that you choose to transfer between paired devices

2. How We Transmit Data

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections to your paired VPS (Virtual Private Server). Message content is end-to-end encrypted (E2EE) between your devices using the Signal Protocol, meaning the server cannot read your message content when E2EE is enabled.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) – used to deliver push notifications to your device. Firebase receives device tokens but not message content.
  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Synced messages, contacts, and call history are stored on the paired VPS server. Data is retained until you delete your account. MMS attachments are stored in cloud storage (Cloudflare R2) linked to your account.

Local data (encrypted database, cached images) is stored on your device and removed when you uninstall the app.

5. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption (E2EE) for messages between paired devices
  • HTTPS for all network communication
  • SQLCipher encrypted local database for sensitive data on-device
  • Biometric authentication option to protect app access

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings within the app.
  • You can unpair devices at any time to stop data synchronization.
  • You can disable optional features (notification mirroring, clipboard sync) at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

SyncFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
